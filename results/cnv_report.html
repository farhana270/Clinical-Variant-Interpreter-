Methods:
Coverage normalisation: Median normalisation corrects for differences in sequencing depth between tumour and normal samples. Log2 ratio = log2(tumour_normalised / normal_normalised).
Segmentation: Simplified circular binary segmentation (CBS) using sliding-window t-tests to identify statistically significant change points (p < 0.01, |t| > 2.0).
CNV thresholds: Deletion log2 < -0.4 | Duplication log2 > 0.3 | Amplification log2 > 1.0
Reference: Olshen AB et al. (2004) Circular binary segmentation for the analysis of array-based DNA copy number data. Biostatistics 5:557–572.
